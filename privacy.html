Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Welcome to Rollapaya. This privacy policy explains how Matthew Fadoul ("I", "me", or "the Developer") handles data within the Rollapaya iOS application.

1. Overview

Rollapaya is designed to be a privacy-focused utility. I do not require user accounts, and I do not collect or store your personal identity, name, or contact information on any private servers.

2. Data Collection and Usage

App Functionality: All app settings and configurations are stored locally on your device or in your private iCloud storage. I do not have access to this data.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep Rollapaya available, I use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide relevant ads and measure performance. This collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

In accordance with Apple's Privacy Labels, the following data may be collected by the AdMob SDK:

  • Identifiers: Device ID (specifically the IDFA for tracking, if permitted).
  • Usage Data: Product interactions and advertising data.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance metrics to ensure stability.
  • Location: Coarse/Approximate location (derived from IP address).

4. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's requirements, the app will request permission to track your activity across other apps and websites for the purpose of personalized advertising. You can manage this choice at any time in your iOS Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. GDPR and International Rights

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, I use a Consent Management Platform (UMP) to obtain explicit consent before initializing personalized advertisements. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time via the in-app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Rollapaya does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. AdMob is configured to respect "Tag for Under Age of Consent" (TFUA) settings where applicable.
